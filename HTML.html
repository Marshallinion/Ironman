OverView

SUIT UP

In a cave in Afghanistan, Tony Stark and Ho Yinsen developed
the first Iron Man suit,which Stark used to escape his captors.

SAVING THE NEW YORK

Fighting alongside the Avengers for the first time,
Iron Man nearly sacrificed himself in order to save New York.

NEAR DEATH

Stark was nearly killed when an attack by the supposed Mandarin destroyed his home,
sending him plummeting into the sea.

SECRETS FROM THE PAST

Already on opposite sides of a fight over the Sokovia Accords, Iron Man unleashed
his full fury on Captain America and Bucky when he learned the truth about his parents's death.

FACING THE MAD TITAN

On the planet Titan, Iron Man, alongside several allies,
took on Thanos in a bid to stop the Titan from gaining all of the Infinity Stones.

I AM IRONMAN

After five years of Thanos snap The avengers collect all the infinity stone by
doing time heist and after an epic battle Ironman kills Thanos and his army with
the help of Infinty Stone and resulting in killing himself too.
